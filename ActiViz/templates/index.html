ActiViz

The activation function is a crucial component of neural networks. It determines how a neuron's output is calculated based on its input. Different activation functions, such as Sigmoid, ReLU, Tanh, and Leaky ReLU, have unique characteristics and are suited for different tasks. This tool allows you to visualize the output of various activation functions for a specific input value.
